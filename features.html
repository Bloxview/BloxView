Features at a Glance

Theater Seating & Showtimes

Choose from Standard, VIP, or Ultra VIP seats—each with unique perks:
• Standard: 25 Robux per seat
• VIP: Priority booking, 10% concession discounts
• Ultra VIP: Private screenings, exclusive collectibles
Scheduled showtimes keep you on time and in sync with friends.

Interactive Lobby & Social Chat

Explore our open-concept lobby for free! Chat with friends, browse trailers, and purchase concessions. The lobby remains active 24/7—join anytime to hang out until your next screening.

Dynamic Lighting & 3D Audio

Immerse yourself with LED ambient lighting that matches each film's mood and 3D spatial audio optimized for high-quality headphones. For best results, set graphics to Max and plug in your headphones.

Group Seating & Seat Gifting

Buy multiple seats at once and share the “seat tool” with friends. Group seating keeps everyone together—no one’s left behind. Or gift seats to friends so they can join you for free.

Community-Driven Creator Network

Apply to join our Creator Network to collaborate on upcoming films. Get priority access to design docs, early asset releases, and join our development Discord. Submit scripts, animations, or sound designs—help shape each film.
